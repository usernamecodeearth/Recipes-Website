Previously Wet Fish
Delicious marine delicacy

What You'll Need:

  1. (Previously wet) Water
  2. Cheese
  3. A lemon from Omega Mart (not to be mistaken for Omega Mart Lemons!)
  4. 2 marine animals of your choosing.
  5. Salt and pepper to taste.

Directions:

Acquire ingredients from your local Omega Mart store and safely return to your dwelling. Once there, soak marine animals of your choosing in previously wet water mixture and let sit for two days, after which you may or may not want to return and procure more ingredients as some have been known to disappear without warning. Next, soak lemon in mixture, place items in heating chamber. Raise the consumable's tempeture to an acceptable degree before enjoying.
